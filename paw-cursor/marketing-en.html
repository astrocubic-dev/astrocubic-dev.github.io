Play Designed for Cats

Animated Friends

Choose a real cursor, laser, fly, ant, ladybug, butterfly, feather, bell, or shooting star, with several friends appearing at once.

Size and Speed Controls

Adjust each friend's visible size, movement speed, play duration, and background color for your cat's vision and preferences.

Cat-Friendly Multitouch

Valid catches still register when both front paws rest on the screen. A large paw mark briefly celebrates each catch.

Camera and Screen Recording

On a real iPad, record your cat and the app screen together, then review both videos with a touch activity timeline.

On-Device Face Privacy

Detected human faces can be covered with a cat-face mask. Face analysis is performed on the device.

Owner Unlock

Before the timer ends, touch and hold with four fingers and optionally enter a passcode to return to the preparation screen.

Safety: Supervise your cat during iPad play. Place the device on a stable surface and consider using a screen protector and sturdy case.

Start a Play Session

1. Choose Friends

Select the friend types, counts, sizes, speed, and play time on the preparation screen.

2. Secure the iPad

Place the iPad where your cat cannot push it over, then enable Guided Access.

3. Play and Revisit

Tap Start Play and supervise. If recording is enabled, review synchronized videos in Play Sessions.
