Stock Certificate Vault

Keep your holdings, share counts, average costs, and current prices as collectible paper-style stock certificates.

Download on the App Store Contact Support Privacy Policy Terms of Use

Key Features

Paper Certificate Vault

Turn your holdings into classic certificate-style cards and organize them as if they were stored in a private vault.

Price Refresh

The app can attempt to refresh current prices for supported listed Korean securities while preserving manually entered values.

Personal Records First

Stock Certificate Vault is for personal records and storage. It does not provide trading, investment advice, or profit guarantees.

The free version can store up to three certificates.
