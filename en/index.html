Long-term Research

Earth Eyes Project

We are exploring a long-term vision for a distributed observatory platform: a network of unmanned telescopes that lets people choose clear skies around the world and access live space data.

This project represents AstroCubic’s long-term vision for remote observation, AI image processing, citizen science, and space data infrastructure.
